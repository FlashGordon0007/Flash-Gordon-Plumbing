Gordon's Hot Water Furnace is the most efficient furnace on the planet! It uses a smart variable speed pump to circulate hot water through a stainless steel radiator inside the furnace cabinet; and a smart variable speed High performance fan. The fan has a soft start so it is silent when it starts, and then every 6 minutes it speeds up it's output by 10%. Once the smart thermostate is satisfied the furnace reverses the proces and starts to reduce it's output by 10% every 6 minutes. The result is a furnace that finds the sweet spot of maintaining the homes desired temperature at the lowest possible speed. The smart thermostate can be operated by any smart phone from anywhere in the world. The free app by Google that comes with the Nest thermostate provides all of the homes energy usage data. These systems typically save the customer 85% in energy costs comparred to their old traditional heating system. The furnace has no burner to leak like traditional furnaces that can cause death by leaking poisionous carbon monoxide gases into the home. This makes these furnaces the safest in the world and their is virtually no mainenance!. They come with a lifetime warranty and only require the filter to be changed saving you thousands in costly maintenance bills.
